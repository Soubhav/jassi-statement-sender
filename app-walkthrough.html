What happens here:

  • Customer list — Shows every customer found in your spreadsheet, their phone number, and their current net balance.
  • Red = owes money — Negative balances are shown in red so you can spot them instantly.
  • Green = in credit — Positive balances appear in green.

  • Pick a customer → Send to Selected — Choose one person from the dropdown and send only to them.
  • Refresh Data — Re-fetches the latest data from Google Sheets without leaving this screen.

Summary

What You Need

Here's everything required to go live — it's a one-time setup that takes about 10 minutes.

1. Google Sheet
Your existing spreadsheet with customer tabs, an "All Accounts" sheet (with phone numbers), and a "Net Balance" sheet. Share it as "Anyone with the link — Viewer".
2. Google API Key (free)
Go to Google Cloud Console → Create a project → Enable "Google Sheets API" → Create an API key under Credentials. No billing needed.
3. That's it.
Open the app, enter the key and sheet ID once, and you're set. Every Sunday, just open the app, click "Fetch", and send.
